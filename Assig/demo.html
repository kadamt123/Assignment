In academic terms, a text is anything that conveys a set of meanings to the person who examines it.
You might have thought that texts were limited to written materials, such as books, magazines,
newspapers, and 'zines (an informal term for magazine that refers especially to fanzines and webzines).
The noun text can be countable or uncountable. In more general, commonly used, contexts, the plural form will also be text.
However, in more specific contexts, the plural form can also be texts e.g. in reference to various types of texts or a collection of texts.
Texting is the same as text messaging.
